Guidelines

General Uniform Good-To Knows:

Never dispose of your uniforms. Uniforms are rented and recyclable. Return them, don't trash them.
Do not take co-worker's uniforms. If it doesn't say your name, it's not your uniform. Notify your supervisor if you don't have any uniforms.
Keep track of your uniforms. Best practice is to launder with Cintas. Report immediately when uniforms are lost, stolen, or just haven't come back to you in a while!
Help Cintas. Our contractors do not field requests on site in order to get through all their deliveries. Please use our RTK email above!
Sometimes it takes longer than expected. Depending on stockroom availability and special size orders (tall sizes, short sizes, etc.), this can take weeks or even months. We're happy to check the status of your order with Cintas for you.
Be specific. The more detail you provide, the quicker we can help you.

Include:

  • Your name as listed on your uniform barcode
  • Where your uniforms get delivered
  • What you want done, and relevant information

Examples:

  • Size change detail (Large to XL)
  • Style change detail (FR? Non-FR? Specific type of pants?)
